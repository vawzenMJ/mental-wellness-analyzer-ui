Mental State Analyzer

⚠️ High Risk Assessment: Seek Support

It's important to reach out for support. Talking to someone can make a significant difference. Consider speaking with a mental health professional.**

Ways to Cope:

  • Talk to a trusted friend or family member about how you're feeling.
  • Practice mindfulness or deep-breathing exercises to manage immediate stress.
  • For immediate support, you can reach out to the **Kenya Red Cross Society's free counseling hotline at 1199.**

Feeling Low? Nurture Your Well-being

It's wonderful that you're feeling well. Keep nurturing your positive mindset and sharing that energy with others.**

Ways to Cope:

  • Continue your healthy habits and positive routines.
  • Consider starting a gratitude journal to focus on the positives in your life.
  • Share your positivity; a simple act of kindness can brighten someone else's day.

✨ Stable Mental State: Maintain Balance

**You're in a balanced state, which is great. Maintaining this equilibrium is key to long-term mental wellness.**

Ways to Cope:

  • Engage in regular physical activity to boost your mood.
  • Ensure you are getting enough sleep and maintaining a balanced diet.
  • Continue to engage in hobbies and activities you enjoy.
